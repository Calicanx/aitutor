### Teaching vs. Content

**Content** is a video you pause, rewind, pause again, then give up on.

**Content** moves at its own pace.

**Content** doesn't know if you understood.

teachr isn't content. It's teaching.

## How It Works

### Simple. Like It Should Be.

**1. Bring Your Problem**
A concept. A homework question. A "why does this even exist" rant. All valid starting points.

**2. Learn Your Way**
Visual learner? We'll draw it out. Need real-world examples? Got those. Want the formal proof? Sure, weirdo, we have that too.

**3. Move On When You're Ready**
Not when the video ends. Not when the bell rings. When you actually get it.
